# Missing-Semester-of-My-CS-Education-at-Tec-
The industry looks for people capable of solving problems. The reality is that even having a master's degree from Tec only shows that you are capable of following instructions, but not thinking critically. 
Following the "The Missing Semester of Your CS Education" MIT style, this repo has everything that they do not teach you in the CS academia. This repo is focused on real-world skills.
